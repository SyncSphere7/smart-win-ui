Pricing FAQs

What does the $100 actually pay for?

The $100 consultation fee opens direct negotiations with our team. It's a filtering mechanism to ensure we work with serious clients only. Actual fixed match arrangements are priced separately based on your specific requirements (league, complexity, risk level, timing).

Why is the fee non-refundable?

The consultation fee grants you access to negotiate with our team, view verified proofs, and review our track record. Once you've gained this access and our team's time, it cannot be revoked, making refunds impossible.

How much do fixed matches actually cost?

Match prices are negotiated individually based on multiple factors: league tier (Premier League vs lower divisions), match complexity, risk level, and timing. We work with various budgets and requirements. Discuss your needs directly with our team after payment.

Can I get free predictions without paying?

Yes! Free prediction samples are available to everyone on our homepage. These demonstrate our 92% success rate and quality. The $100 fee is only required to negotiate custom fixed match arrangements.

Is there a monthly subscription?

No. The $100 is a one-time payment for lifetime negotiation rights. Each fixed match is then priced separately based on your agreement with our team.

How secure are the payment methods?

All card and mobile money payments are processed through Pesapal, a PCI DSS compliant payment gateway. Cryptocurrency transactions are secured by blockchain technology.
